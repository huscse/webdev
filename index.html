Whack a Professor

Game Description

Welcome to "Whack-a-Professor" This is a fast-paced casual game designed to test the player's reaction speed and hand-eye coordination. The goal is to hit the moles (professors) as they appear randomly.

Each professor appears for a limited time, and you need to click on them quickly to score points.

Rules

  • Click on professors as they appear to earn points.
  • The game lasts for 60 seconds.
  • The time the mole is visible decreases as the game progresses.
  • The player with the highest score wins.

How to Play

  1. Click the "Start Game" button below to begin.
  2. Use your mouse/trackpad to click on professors.
  3. Try to click as many professors as possible before time runs out.
  4. Keep an eye on your score at the top of the game screen.
  5. When the game ends, you'll see your final score.
